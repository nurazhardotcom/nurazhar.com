The Agile Manifesto (2001) defines 12 principles that every Scrum framework is built on. Here they are in plain language.

---

## The Principles

| # | Principle | What It Means |
|---|-----------|---------------|
| **1** | Customer satisfaction through early delivery | Ship value every Sprint |
| **2** | Welcome changing requirements | Backlog is never frozen |
| **3** | Deliver working software frequently | Increment every Sprint |
| **4** | Business people & devs work together daily | PO sits with the team |
| **5** | Build around motivated individuals. Trust them. | Self-managing, no micromanagement |
| **6** | Face-to-face is most efficient | 15-min standup beats 10 Slack threads |
| **7** | Working software is primary progress measure | Not Gantt charts. Does the Increment work? |
| **8** | Sustainable pace indefinitely | No death marches |
| **9** | Continuous technical excellence | DoD, refactoring, testing |
| **10** | Simplicity — maximize work not done | YAGNI. Build only what you need now. |
| **11** | Self-organizing teams = best architectures | Devs decide how. No PM assigns tasks. |
| **12** | Regular reflection, tune and adjust | The Sprint Retrospective |

---

## The 4 That Matter Most

**#12 — The Retrospective**
This is the Sprint Retrospective. *"At regular intervals, the team reflects on how to become more effective, then tunes and adjusts."* If you memorize one principle, make it this one.

**#2 — Embrace Change**
*"Welcome changing requirements, even late in development."* The Product Backlog exists because of this principle. Change is fuel, not friction.

**#4 — Daily Collaboration**
*"Business people and developers must work together daily."* The Product Owner is part of the team, not a distant stakeholder.

**#7 — Working Software as Progress**
*"Working software is the primary measure of progress."* Not slide decks. Not documentation. A usable Increment every Sprint.

---

## One-Liner

The 12 principles are the Manifesto's *how* — Scrum is the framework that makes that *how* repeatable across every Sprint.
